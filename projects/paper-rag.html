Paper-RAG

AI Co-researcher for Full-cycle Academic Writing

Multi-Agent RAG LLM Research Ops

Overview

Paper-RAG is an AI Co-researcher System designed to support the entire research lifecycle. It automates the workflow from literature acquisition and analysis to related research design, drafting, and quality assurance, enabling researchers to focus on creative discovery.

Problem

Researchers spend excessive time on manual literature review, synthesis, and formatting, which limits the resources available for critical thinking and creative research design.

Paper-RAG addresses this with a comprehensive 5-Phase Agent Workflow:

  • Multi-Agent System: Specialized agents (Research Design, Draft, Style Check) for each phase.
  • Contextual RAG: Evidence-based generation with precise citation anchoring.
  • Automated QA: Real-time verification for citations, style, and consistency.

Architecture

Component Technology
Dataset PDF Papers + Targeted Retrieval (arXiv/OpenAlex)
LLM & VLM Upstage Solar Pro, Qwen 2.5 (Agent), Gemini Flash (Vision)
Retrieval Hybrid (Dense + BM25) + Hierarchical Chunking
Generator Section-based Fusion Writer (APA 7th Style)
Evaluation RAGAS Metrics + Citation/Style Verification Agents

Key Features

  • Phase 1 (Research Design): Deep PDF parsing & VLM analysis, automated Gap Analysis, and RQ formulation.
  • Phase 2 (Draft): Citation-backed section writing using Solar Pro models.
  • Phase 3 (Style Check): Automated verification of citations, academic style, and terminology.

Vision

Paper-RAG embodies the motto "From Data to Discovery", redefining how research takes shape by fostering collaboration between human creativity and AI efficiency.

"An end-to-end AI partner that transforms how we discover, design, and write research."
