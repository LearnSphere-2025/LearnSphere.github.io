Paper-RAG

AI Assistant for Academic Writing and Literature Synthesis

RAG Research LLM Education AI

Overview

Paper-RAG is a research automation system that assists scholars and independent researchers in literature retrieval, synthesis, and structured writing.

It uses retrieval-augmented generation to ground each writing suggestion in peer-reviewed research.

Problem

Researchers spend excessive time searching, summarizing, and contextualizing existing studies — tasks that can be automated without losing academic rigor.

Paper-RAG addresses this by integrating:

  • Contextual retrieval (BM25 + Sentence Embeddings)
  • Claim-based summarization
  • Citation-aware text generation

Architecture

Component Technology
Dataset Curated academic corpus (Scopus + Semantic Scholar API)
Embedding Model upskyy/bge-m3-korean for bilingual search
Generator Domain-adaptive LLM fine-tuned for structured abstracts
Evaluation Semantic similarity + RAGAS benchmarking

Key Features

  • Structured Literature Retrieval: Clustered by topic and research question
  • Citation Anchoring: Every claim linked to source metadata
  • Outline Assistance: Auto-generate research gaps, implications, and summaries

Vision

Paper-RAG is a foundation for AI-assisted scholarly creativity, helping independent researchers like those at LearnSphere to produce high-quality academic work efficiently.

"From data to discovery — Paper-RAG redefines how research takes shape in the age of AI."
