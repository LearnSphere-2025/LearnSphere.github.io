HR-RAG

Retrieval-Augmented AI for Automated HR Reports

RAG LLM HR Automation

Overview

HR-RAG is a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system designed to support HR analysts and compliance researchers in automating policy report generation.

It connects a curated knowledge base of labor law documents, HR guidelines, and precedent reports with large language models optimized for structured reasoning.

Objectives

  • Reduce manual drafting time for HR and compliance documents
  • Improve consistency and legal accuracy in report writing
  • Provide explainable outputs with document traceability

Architecture

  • Retrieval Layer: Dense and hybrid search (BM25 + Sentence Transformer)
  • Augmentation Layer: Context packaging with citation tagging
  • Generation Layer: Fine-tuned LLM with HR-specific prompt templates
