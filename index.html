About LearnSphere

LearnSphere is an independent AI research lab founded by Rora, an education researcher and data scientist.

The lab explores how artificial intelligence can enhance research, automate knowledge work, and personalize learning across domains.

Our current focus is on applied RAG systems and educational AI models that bridge academic research and practical impact.
