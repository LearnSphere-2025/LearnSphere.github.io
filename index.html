About LearnSphere

LearnSphere is an independent AI research lab founded by an education researcher dedicated to exploring the intersection of learning, data, and technology.

The lab investigates how artificial intelligence can enhance research, automate knowledge creation, and personalize learning experiences across domains.

Currently, LearnSphere focuses on developing applied RAG systems and educational AI models that connect academic insight with real-world innovation.

Vision

LearnSphere envisions a world where education, data, and AI converge to transform knowledge into human-centered innovation.
